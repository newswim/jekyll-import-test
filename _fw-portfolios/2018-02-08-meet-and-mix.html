Meet & Mix

SUMMARY

We decided one big group event per year isn't enough! That's why we're adding a spring event to bring all of Denton's tech community together.

The first annual Meet & Mix will be held Thursday, March 8, 2018 at 6:30 PM at Denton County Brewing Company, 200 East McKinney Street. Everyone who is part of Denton's tech community or would like to be is welcome to join us!

Looking to hire technical talent? Meet & Mix is for you.

Looking for your next career opportunity in tech? Meet & Mix is for you.

Have you thought about learning to code but are afraid it might be too hard? You guessed it - Meet & Mix is for you!

Meet & Mix is a free event, but RSVPs are appreciated (click to get your tickets). In honor of our new status as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, the first 25 people to make a donation will get a TechMill pint glass at the event!

By the way, being an event sponsor is a great way to promote your business to our local tech community! If you're interested in being a sponsor for Meet & Mix, email us at techmilldenton {at} gmail {dot} com.
