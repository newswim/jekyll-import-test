Thanks to the passionate support of the Denton startup community, we are excited to announce that TechMill is expanding!

We are now at 100 W. Oak Street, #200, Denton, Texas 76201. That’s right on the square! 

There are a host of new amenities members will now have access to:

  • A large 8-10 person private conference room

  • A private phone room for calls

  • General seating area

  • Repurposed wood desks custom made for us by Denton Sustainable Creations

  • A kitchen with refrigerator, microwave, bar-height table and chairs, and Keurig coffee maker

  • Laser printing and scanning with free supplies (paper / toner / ink)

  • Integrated power and Ethernet at every desk pod

  • 8AM-6PM access M-F, and 24/7 access coming hopefully by the end of the year

Starting today, we will be offering 2 membership levels:

  • Regular membership: $150/mo, Access to any desk with all amenities listed above
  • Townie membership: $200/mo, Access to a private desk for equipment, with all amenities listed above

Along with the new space, we have just signed two new Community Partners! Hayes, Berry, White & Vanzant LLP is offering 10 hours of legal counsel, and Bookkeeper Girl will be offering 50% off book keeping services for members of our space!

Join our meetup group, or like us on Facebook!

This growth couldn’t have happened without every single one of you, and for that we thank you, from the bottom of our hearts.
