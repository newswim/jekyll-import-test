We are proud to announce that we've added Danielle Gaither to the TechMill Board.

Danielle has been an enthusiastic member of the Denton tech community for years. She is a PhD candidate at UNT, content writer, photographer, software developer, and military veteran. Her diverse and extensive knowledge will be a valuable asset in our mission to increase the tech density in Denton.

Danielle is replacing Dave Sims who is busy working with Kubos on sending things to space and putting Denton on the satellite technology map.
